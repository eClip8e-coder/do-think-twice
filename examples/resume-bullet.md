> Built internal dashboard that reduced weekly reporting time from 4 hours to 30 minutes for the operations team.

## Without Do Think Twice

> Spearheaded the development of a mission-critical analytics platform, leveraging cross-functional stakeholder alignment to optimize operational reporting workflows by 87.5%.

## With Do Think Twice

- Overall judgment: The original is strong because it is concrete and quantified.
- Must fix: None.
- Strongly recommended: If accurate, add the tool or audience only if it helps match the target role.
- Optional rewrite: "Built an internal operations dashboard that cut weekly reporting time from 4 hours to 30 minutes."
- Not recommended: Do not inflate "dashboard" into "mission-critical analytics platform" unless that is accurate. Do not add vague phrases like "stakeholder alignment" unless they reflect a real responsibility.

The guided response keeps the credibility and measurable result of the original, while making only a small clarity improvement.
